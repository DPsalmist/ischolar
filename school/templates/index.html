Welcome Address

A very warm and happy welcome to New World -Scholars learning community.
New World I-Scholars is a private co-educational institution for Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) and Primary Years. It is an integrated curriculum school (British and Nigerian). Established in 2018, adopting a pupil-centered activity based teaching.

New World I-Scholars is clearly a school where the desire to learn is infectious and students of all abilities flourish because everyone works hard to create an enabling learning environment, where everybody is welcome and diversity is celebrated.
Everything at New World I-scholars is designed to encourage students and pupils to take an active role in their learning. We don't just teach, we inspire.
At New World I-Scholars, learning takes place both in the classroom and outside it. Our co-curricular programme offers a wide range of sporting, cultural and creative activities to suit every taste. In addition to educational visits locally.
While the wriiten word will give you a small sense of what a wonderful school New World I-Scholars is, whether you are thinking of joining us or are already a member of the family, it can never convey the whole picture. Therefore, I warmly invite you to visit us and see for yourselves how engaging, confident, balanced and polite our students/pupils are.
You are welcome to see us at anytime. We look forward to meeting you.
