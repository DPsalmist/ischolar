About Us

New World I-Scholars is an integrated curriculum school (British and Nigerian). It offers the British national curriculum and the Nigerian curriculum. The Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) is strictly British based while the Primary and the secondary years is a mixture of the British curriculum and the Nigerian curriculum where we strive to build up a 21st century child.

New World I-Scholars attracts families seeking rigorous academics, a whole child philosophy, and a warm and nuturing environment for their children.
